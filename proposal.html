01

Executive summary

Today your service requests arrive by phone and live in people's heads, notebooks, and chat groups. There's no single place to see who's doing what, where, and whether it's actually done.

ServiceHub replaces that with one connected system across four roles — Customer, Technician, Project Manager, and Admin. A missed call becomes a ticket; the manager assigns it; the technician does the work and files a mandatory activity report with photos; the manager approves and closes it. Every job is tracked across your Calicut, Kollam and Ernakulam cost centers, with proof of work on record.

We propose to build this in two phases — a working core first, then a fully customized version shaped by your feedback — for a total of ₹3,25,000.

04

Scope, in two phases

Phase 01

Core Workflow Platform

Build first

The working spine — live and usable, end to end.

  • Secure login & roles (Customer / Technician / PM / Admin)
  • Ticket creation, classification & assignment
  • Technician app (web/mobile) — today's tasks & status
  • Daily Activity Report + site photo upload
  • PM approvals — approve or request changes
  • Admin console — tickets, technicians, cost centers
  • Availability & cross-branch deployment view
  • Deployed to the cloud on your domain
Phase 02

Full Customized Version

After feedback

Shaped by how your team actually uses Phase 1.

  • Missed-call → automatic ticket & SMS link
  • Notifications — SMS, push & email alerts
  • Full project tracking — milestones & progress
  • Customization to your exact process & forms
  • Reports & analytics dashboards
  • Native mobile app (Play Store / App Store)
  • Offline support for field technicians
  • Security hardening, training & handover
